Core Lesson (10–15 minutes)

  • HIV Basics: CD4 T-cells, ART suppresses virus, U=U for sexual transmission when undetectable.
  • Linkage/Retention: same-day connect, address SDOH, transport plan, reminders, peer groups.
  • Communication: people-first language, ask permission, reflective listening, cultural humility.
  • Prevention: PrEP (before exposure), PEP (≤72h, 28 days), condoms, STI testing, harm reduction.
  • Teamwork & Documentation: factual notes, confidentiality, coordinate across disciplines.
  • Self-Care: supervision, peer support, boundaries.
